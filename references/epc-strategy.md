# EPC Strategy Reference

## Bid Positioning

For Kazakhstan/Central Asia HPP tenders, avoid accepting unlimited lump-sum turnkey exposure for risks that are not controlled by the EPC contractor.

Protect the bid with:

- Geotechnical baseline report (GBR) as the risk boundary.
- Clear hydrology baseline for diversion, cofferdams, floods, and access disruption.
- Employer responsibility for permits, land, grid right-of-way, and water allocation unless explicitly priced.
- Compensation event for change in law, authority delay, late land handover, grid interface delay, archaeology, unexploded ordnance, and materially adverse environmental restrictions.
- EOT and cost compensation for abnormal floods beyond design construction criteria.

## Technical Tender Focus

Prioritize:

- Diversion tunnel and cofferdam sequence.
- RCC temperature control, aggregate source, batching capacity, lift schedule, galleries, grouting.
- CFRD rockfill zoning, face slab joints, plinth grouting, settlement control.
- Spillway hydraulics and energy dissipation.
- Tunnel support class, NATM method statement, ventilation, mucking, water ingress.
- Francis turbine efficiency, cavitation margin, governor response, AGC interface, protection/control compatibility with KEGOC.
- Winter construction plan, mountain access, logistics, camp, concrete supply, explosives, customs.

## Commercial and Claims Checklist

Track:

- Baseline vs actual geology.
- Design changes issued after bid.
- Late approval of drawings, permits, grid interface, land, quarry, access roads.
- Environmental flow or biodiversity measures added after contract.
- Inflation, FX, customs, sanctions/export restrictions.
- Employer-caused suspension or delayed commissioning tests.
- Performance test constraints caused by hydrology, grid, or dispatch instructions.

## Preferred EPC Offer

Offer a package that combines:

- EPC construction.
- Owner's engineer/design optimization support where allowed.
- Commissioning and grid-code compliance testing.
- O&M training and spare parts.
- Optional 2-5 year O&M advisory or availability-support package.
